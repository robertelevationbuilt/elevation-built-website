Specialty Batten Systems & Architectural Features

Elevation Built installs timber and metal batten systems, acoustic screens, feature walls and custom architectural elements for high-end commercial fitouts across Adelaide and South Australia. Battens are one of the most visible and detail-sensitive elements in a commercial interior — and we treat them accordingly, with precise spacing, consistent profiles and finishes that honour the design intent.

From vertical timber batten screens in childcare centres across greater Adelaide to horizontal metal batten ceilings in corporate headquarters, our team delivers the quality and consistency that architects and Tier 1 builders in South Australia expect.

Why Elevation Built

Architectural Detail, Consistently Delivered

Batten work demands meticulous set-out, consistent gaps and a finish that survives scrutiny. Our teams are trained to deliver exactly that — every time, across Adelaide and SA.

Precision Spacing

Set-out engineered to match the approved drawings — consistent gaps throughout, no drift from first batten to last, across every room and every level.

Custom Profiles

Timber or metal, standard or custom — we work to your specification or direct from the architect's schedule, sourcing and machining profiles to suit each project.

Acoustic Performance

Batten wall and ceiling systems designed and installed to meet specified NRC acoustic targets — critical in healthcare, education and corporate environments across South Australia.

Premium Timber

Timber selected and stored correctly to minimise movement after installation — seasoned, graded and finished to the standard expected in high-end Adelaide commercial fitouts.

Metal Finished

Powder-coated or anodised metal battens installed to a commercial standard — sharp lines, flush ends and consistent colour throughout each system delivered in SA.

Design Intent Delivered
