Who We Are

Built on Accountability, Systems & Precision Delivery

Elevation Built is an Adelaide-based commercial subcontractor specialising in ceilings, partitions, carpentry, external cladding and specialty batten systems. We partner exclusively with Tier 1 builders across South Australia — including SHAPE Australia, Mossop Construction and Chappell Construction — delivering the programme certainty, compliance rigour and variation management that major principal contractors require.

Our business is built on 16 documented Standard Operating Procedures that govern how we win work, plan projects, manage sites, control variations and process claims. Every project runs on a live 4-week lookahead programme, reviewed weekly with the principal contractor and updated to reflect current site conditions across Adelaide and regional South Australia.

Licensed supervisors and certified crews on every project. Zero tolerance for safety non-conformance. A culture of ownership — from Director to site. That's the standard at Elevation Built.

How We Work

How We Operate

Three disciplines that drive every Elevation Built project in South Australia — from first contact to final payment.

Win the Work

Estimating from first principles. Competitive pricing from our SA-calibrated rate library. Relationships built over time with Adelaide's Tier 1 builders. We tender smart, price to win, and qualify our scope clearly up front — no surprises on either side.

Deliver the Work

4-week lookahead planning, daily site management by certified supervisors, and proactive RFI resolution. Safety-first culture with current SWMS and prestarts on every Adelaide and SA site. We deliver to programme and hand over clean — every time.

Get Paid

Progress claims submitted on time and in full. Every variation identified, costed and submitted — nothing falls off the scope. Payment claims managed systematically by our Contract Administrator so Robert can focus on the work, not the paperwork.
