About Us

Adelaide's Commercial Construction Specialists

A specialist commercial subcontractor delivering ceilings, partitions, carpentry, cladding and batten systems to the highest standard across South Australia.

Our Story

Built on Systems.
Delivered on Programme.

Elevation Built was founded on a single conviction: that commercial construction subcontracting in South Australia deserved better. Better systems, better communication, better accountability — and a team that Tier 1 builders could genuinely rely on.

We operate across four trade disciplines — ceilings and partitions, external cladding, bespoke carpentry, and specialty batten systems — delivering every project under licensed supervision with a documented 16-SOP operational framework that removes guesswork and eliminates surprises.

Our values are simple. Show up on programme. Document everything. Take ownership of the commercial position. And build long-term partnerships with the builders who trust us with their most demanding projects.
