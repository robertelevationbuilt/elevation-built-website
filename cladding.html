02 — Façade Systems

Façades built to
perform and comply.

Elevation Built installs fibre cement cladding and architectural façade systems for commercial buildings across South Australia. Our crews work to manufacturer specifications and NCC Volume One requirements — delivering weathertight, fire-compliant façades that stand up to the SA climate and regulatory scrutiny.

We take on cladding packages as part of broader fitout scopes or as standalone façade works. Our system includes full material handling, substrate preparation, fixing to engineered specifications, and joint sealing — coordinated within the builder's programme.

Every cladding installation is backed by compliance documentation maintained by our Contract Administrator, ensuring the façade system meets the project's fire performance, weatherproofing and NCC obligations without exception.

Capabilities

What we install.

CFC Cladding

Compressed fibre cement cladding boards installed to manufacturer specification across commercial façades — durable, low-maintenance, and suited to South Australia's climate conditions.

NCC Compliance

All cladding installations meet NCC Volume One requirements. Full compliance documentation is prepared and maintained by our Contract Administrator throughout the project lifecycle.

Fire-Rated Systems

Fire-rated cladding assemblies installed to meet BAL and FRL requirements — tested systems, engineered details and full compliance records for Certifier submission.

Engineered Fixings

All fixing systems are specified to structural engineer requirements. No shortcuts — every anchor, bracket and fastener is installed to the engineer's specification and documented.
