Why Elevation Built

Standards-Driven Cladding Installation

External cladding carries significant compliance obligations. We manage every layer of the process — from NCC documentation through to weathertight certification.

NCC Compliance

All cladding installations meet NCC Volume One requirements — documentation prepared and maintained throughout the project for inspections and certification in Adelaide SA.

Weathertight Systems

Every penetration sealed, every junction detailed — our installations are weathertight from day one, protecting the building envelope for the long term in South Australia's climate.

Fire Rated Options

Certified installation of fire-rated cladding systems for healthcare, education and multi-storey commercial projects requiring fire-performance façade solutions across SA.

Structural Fixing

All fixing systems engineered to specification — correct anchor types, load-rated fixings and substrate preparation for lasting, safe cladding performance.

Sealed & Finished

Sealant application, flashing, trim and corner details completed to a commercial standard — façades handed over clean and complete, ready for final inspection.

Tier 1 Tested

Proven experience delivering external cladding packages for South Australia's major principal contractors — systems, compliance documentation and reporting all in place.
